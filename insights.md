## Key Findings
- High-risk loan grades show significantly higher default rates.
- A small portion of loans contributes to a large share of default exposure.
- Medium-risk loans represent the largest volume but lower default percentage.

## Business Recommendations
- Tighten approval criteria for high-risk grades.
- Increase monitoring of medium-risk loans.
- Use risk-grade segmentation to limit total exposure.
